Paris Travel Page

Paris, the capital of France, is a city of romance and beauty. From the iconic Eiffel Tower to the artistic masterpieces of the Louvre, Paris has something for everyone. Explore the charming streets of Montmartre, visit the Palace of Versailles, or take a stroll along the Seine River. Whether you're a foodie, a history buff, or an art lover, Paris is a city that will leave you enchanted.

Top attractions

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is an iconic iron lattice tower built for the 1889 World's Fair. It stands 324 meters tall and offers breathtaking views of the city.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing a vast collection of art and artifacts from ancient civilizations to the 19th century.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Notre-Dame Cathedral is a beautiful Gothic church built in the 12th century. It was the site of many royal coronations and is one of the most famous churches in the world.
