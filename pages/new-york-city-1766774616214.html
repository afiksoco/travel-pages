New York City

New York City is a bustling metropolis known for its iconic skyline, world-class museums, and diverse neighborhoods. From the bright lights of Times Square to the peaceful green oasis of Central Park, there's something for everyone in the city that never sleeps. Whether you're interested in history, art, food, or entertainment, New York City has it all.

Top attractions

Statue of Liberty

Take a ferry to Liberty Island to see the iconic Statue of Liberty up close. Learn about its history and significance at the on-site museum.

Central Park

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city with a stroll through Central Park. This 843-acre green space offers walking paths, lakes, and plenty of people-watching opportunities.

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

With over 2 million works of art spanning 5,000 years of human history, The Met is one of the world's greatest museums. From ancient Egyptian artifacts to modern masterpieces, there's always something new to discover.
