New York City

New York City is a bustling metropolis known for its iconic skyline, world-class museums, and diverse cultural scene. From the bright lights of Times Square to the peaceful green oasis of Central Park, NYC has something for everyone. With its rich history, vibrant neighborhoods, and endless entertainment options, it's no wonder NYC is one of the world's most popular tourist destinations.

Top attractions

Statue of Liberty

Take a ferry to Liberty Island to see the iconic Statue of Liberty up close. This national monument is a symbol of freedom and democracy, and offers stunning views of the Manhattan skyline.

Central Park

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city with a stroll through Central Park. This 843-acre green space offers walking paths, lakes, and plenty of picnic spots.

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

With over 2 million works of art spanning 5,000 years of human history, the Met is one of the world's greatest museums. From ancient Egyptian artifacts to modern masterpieces, there's something for every art lover.
