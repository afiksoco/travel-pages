Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is a city of love and romance. It is known for its stunning architecture, art museums, and fashion. The city is home to the famous Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. Visitors can enjoy a romantic river cruise or a stroll through the beautiful gardens of the Palace of Versailles.

Top attractions

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a iconic iron lattice tower built in 1889. It stands 324 meters tall and offers stunning views of the city from its observation decks.

The Louvre Museum

The Louvre is one of the world's largest and most famous museums. It houses a vast collection of art and artifacts from around the world, including the Mona Lisa.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Notre-Dame Cathedral is a beautiful Gothic church built in the 12th century. It was one of the most famous landmarks in Paris before a fire damaged the cathedral in 2019.
