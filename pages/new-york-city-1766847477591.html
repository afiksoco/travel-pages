New York City

New York City, the most populous city in the United States, is a hub for culture, entertainment, and finance. From iconic landmarks like the Statue of Liberty and Central Park to world-class museums like the Met and MoMA, NYC has something for everyone. Whether you're a foodie, a shopaholic, or an art lover, NYC is a city that never sleeps. With its diverse neighborhoods and endless possibilities, NYC is a must-visit destination for any traveler.

Top attractions

Statue of Liberty

Take a ferry to Liberty Island to see the iconic Statue of Liberty up close. Learn about its history and significance at the nearby museum.

Central Park

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city in this 843-acre green oasis. Take a stroll, have a picnic, or rent a bike and explore the park's many trails.

Metropolitan Museum of Art

With over 2 million works of art spanning 5,000 years of human history, the Met is one of the world's greatest museums. From ancient Egyptian artifacts to modern masterpieces, there's something for every art lover.
