Paris

Top 5 Attractions in Paris

  • The Eiffel Tower: The iconic iron lattice tower offering stunning views of the city.
  • The Louvre Museum: One of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing an impressive collection of art and artifacts.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral: A beautiful and historic Catholic cathedral that was built in the 12th century.
  • Arc de Triomphe: A monumental arch honoring the soldiers who fought and died for France.
  • Montmartre: A historic and charming neighborhood with narrow streets, artist studios, and stunning views of the city.
