Paris

Top 5 Attractions in Paris

  1. The Eiffel Tower

    The Eiffel Tower is an iconic symbol of Paris and one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world. Built for the 1889 World's Fair, it stands at an impressive 324 meters tall and offers breathtaking views of the city.

  2. The Louvre Museum

    The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing an impressive collection of art and artifacts from around the world, including the Mona Lisa.

  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral

    Notre-Dame Cathedral is a beautiful and historic church that dates back to the 12th century. It's known for its stunning architecture and stunning stained-glass windows.

  4. Arc de Triomphe

    The Arc de Triomphe is a monumental arch that honors the soldiers who fought and died for France. It's located in the center of the famous Champs-Élysées avenue and offers great views of the city.

  5. Montmartre

    Montmartre is a historic and artistic neighborhood that's known for its bohemian vibe, street artists, and stunning views of the city. It's a great place to explore and get a feel for the local culture.
