Welcome to Rome

Rome, the Eternal City, is a must-visit destination for any traveler. With its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture, Rome has something to offer for everyone.

From ancient ruins to baroque fountains, Rome is a city that will leave you in awe. Here are the top 5 attractions to visit in Rome:

  1. The Colosseum: This ancient amphitheater is one of Rome's most iconic landmarks and a must-visit attraction. Learn about the history of gladiators and the Roman Empire while exploring the ancient ruins.
  2. The Vatican City: The center of Catholicism, the Vatican City is home to numerous iconic landmarks, including St. Peter's Basilica, the Sistine Chapel, and the Vatican Museums.
  3. The Pantheon: This ancient temple is one of the best-preserved ancient buildings in Rome and is a must-visit for history buffs and architecture enthusiasts.
  4. The Roman Forum: Once the heart of ancient Rome, the Roman Forum is now a vast archaeological site featuring ancient ruins, temples, and basilicas.
  5. The Spanish Steps: One of Rome's most famous landmarks, the Spanish Steps are a beautiful example of baroque architecture and a great place to people-watch and enjoy the city's vibrant atmosphere.

We hope you enjoy your trip to Rome!
