Welcome to Rome

Rome, the Eternal City, is a must-visit destination for any traveler. With its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture, Rome has something to offer for everyone.

From ancient ruins to beautiful piazzas, Rome is a city that will leave you in awe. Here are the top 5 attractions to visit in Rome:

  1. Colosseum: This ancient amphitheater is one of Rome's most iconic landmarks and a must-visit attraction. Learn about the history of gladiators and the Roman Empire while exploring the ancient structure.
  2. Vatican City: The center of the Catholic Church, Vatican City is home to numerous iconic landmarks, including St. Peter's Basilica, the Sistine Chapel, and the Vatican Museums.
  3. Roman Forum: Once the heart of ancient Rome, the Roman Forum is now a sprawling archaeological site featuring ancient ruins, temples, and basilicas.
  4. Pantheon: This stunning temple is one of the best-preserved ancient buildings in Rome and features an impressive dome designed by Michelangelo.
  5. Trevi Fountain: Toss a coin into the beautiful Trevi Fountain, which is said to guarantee your return to Rome. The fountain is a masterpiece of baroque architecture and features intricate carvings and statues.
