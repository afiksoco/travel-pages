Welcome to Rome, the Eternal City

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city steeped in history, art, and architecture. From ancient ruins to beautiful piazzas, Rome has something to offer for every kind of traveler.

One of the most recognizable landmarks in Rome is the Colosseum, a ancient amphitheater that hosted gladiator battles and public events.

Another notable attraction in Rome is the Pantheon, a magnificently preserved ancient temple with an impressive dome.

Be sure to visit the Trevi Fountain, a beautiful baroque fountain where you can make a wish by throwing a coin into the water.

Top 5 Attractions in Rome

  1. Colosseum: This ancient amphitheater is a must-visit attraction in Rome, offering a glimpse into the city's rich history.
  2. Vatican City: The center of Catholicism, Vatican City is home to numerous iconic landmarks, including St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel.
  3. Roman Forum: Once the heart of ancient Rome, the Roman Forum features a collection of ruins, including temples, basilicas, and markets.
  4. Pantheon: This ancient temple is one of the best-preserved ancient buildings in Rome, boasting an impressive dome and oculus.
  5. Spanish Steps: One of the city's most famous landmarks, the Spanish Steps offer a beautiful spot to people-watch and take in the city's vibrant atmosphere.
