Welcome to Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv is a vibrant city located on the Mediterranean coast of Israel. It is known for its beautiful beaches, rich history, and vibrant cultural scene.

Here are the top 5 attractions to visit in Tel Aviv:

  1. Carmel Market: A bustling marketplace filled with fresh produce, spices, and souvenirs.
  2. Tel Aviv Museum of Art: A world-class museum featuring a collection of contemporary and modern art.
  3. Jaffa Old City: A historic neighborhood filled with narrow streets, charming shops, and ancient architecture.
  4. Palmach Museum: A museum dedicated to the history of the Palmach, a Jewish military force that played a significant role in Israel's war of independence.
  5. White City: A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring over 4,000 buildings built in the Bauhaus style.
